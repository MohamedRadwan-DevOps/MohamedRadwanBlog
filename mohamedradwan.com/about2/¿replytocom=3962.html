About Mohamed Radwan

[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner css=”.vc_custom_1463735836515{background-color: #f5f5f5 !important;}”][vc_column_inner el_class=”new_about”][vc_column_text]

Mohamed Radwan is a Developer Technologies MVP formerly known as (ALM MVP), Mohamed is working as Principal DevOps and Agile Consultant; his work focuses on providing solutions for the various roles involved in software development and delivery through Agile Methodologies and utilization of Azure DevOps/Cloud Tools & Technologies.

 

He has huge passion for automation with 15+ years of professional experience spanning the different stages and phases of Software Development Lifecycle. he has helped various customers around the globe in the UK, Denmark, USA, France, Egypt, Oman, KSA, Kuwait, and Libya, amongst others.

 

He is specialised in Agile Methodologies and cloud solutions, and frequent speaker at many national and regional technology events and user groups for various countries, such as: Microsoft Tech Days UK, Campus Days in Denmark, Copenhagen user group, Open Days in Egypt, KSA, Kuwait, and Oman. he has authored several frameworks and extensions including DevMagicFake, TestingConf Utilities and many others, as well as many technical  guides & articles. He is the founder of the TFSEG and co-founder of the MEAALM Community.

 

Mohamed holds a number of Microsoft certificates include Azure DevOps, Azure Architect, MCT, MCPD, MCITP in EPM, MCTS (7), MCSD, MCAD, and CIW among others.
